COMTECH

COMTECH is the official academic club representing undergraduate students of the Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics (FSKM) at Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT). Our vibrant community brings together students from three specialized programs:

  • Bachelor of Computer Science (Software Engineering) with Honours
  • Bachelor of Computer Science with Maritime Informatics (Honours)
  • Bachelor of Computer Science (Mobile Computing) with Honours

COMTECH fosters collaboration, innovation, and personal growth through tech-driven events, workshops, and student-led initiatives. We aim to empower future technologists by creating a space where ideas thrive and skills evolve.

Our History

The history of the academic club, COMTECH, traces back to the 1990s when Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) was known as Universiti Pertanian Malaysia Terengganu (UPMT). Established during this period, COMTECH initially comprised of undergraduate students from the computer science program who were known for their skills, talent, ambition, and drive.

Over the years, COMTECH has evolved into a prominent platform for collaboration, innovation, and knowledge exchange among aspiring technologists. With a strong emphasis on fostering academic excellence, professional development, and community engagement, COMTECH endeavors to empower its members to excel in the dynamic field of computer science while making meaningful contributions to society.

Throughout its journey, COMTECH has been led by dedicated coordinators, including Dr. Noor Azliza binti Che Mat, followed by Associate Professor Dr. Engku Fadzli Hassan bin Syed Abdullah, and currently overseen by Dr. Khairi.

Our Objectives

The COMTECH club aims to achieve the vision and mission, which is “Empowering Future Tech Leaders” by “Empowering a highly skilled, competitive, and ethical generation to strengthen the community.”

Each program conducted by this club not only aims to enhance the reputation of the club, faculty, and university, but also aspires to cultivate respectful and highly skilled technology leaders.
